Payment Summary Info

You can now keep track of your earnings and Lynk payments from the Lynk Driver's App. Here's the list of terminologies used and their definitions:

  1. Opening Balance:

    2. This is the amount that is trailing from your previous payment cycle. A positive opening balance indicates that there is a payment due from Lynk to you, and a negative opening balance indicates that there is collection due from you towards Lynk.

  2. Cash Collected:

    3. This is total amount of cash that you have collected from the customers from the cash trips.

  3. Your Earning (Cash Trips):

    4. This is your total earnings from the cash trips

  4. Your Earning (Credit Trips):

    5. This is your total earnings from the credit trips.

  5. Incentive:

    6. This is your total earnings from Lynk Incentives.

  6. Penalty:

    7. This is the total amount of penalty.

  7. Net Payable/Receivable:

    8. This is the net amount (after adjusting your earnings, incentives and penalties with the cash collected and opening balance) that is either payable/receivable.

  8. Payment Made:

    9. This is the net amount that has been paid towards the net payable/receivable for this cycle.

  9. Closing Balance:

    10. This is the amount is not settled in the current payment cycle and will roll over to your next cycle. A positive closing balance indicated that there is payment due from Lynk to you, and a negative closing balance indicates that there is a collection due from you to towards.

Note:

Payments for trips done today will be processed by 3 PM tomorrow.
